Meet Our Brands

The brand house.

Cannabis Brothers International

Decades of expertise.
World-renowned genetics.

Founded by brothers Terence and Philip Cabral, The Cannabis Brothers have been cultivating cannabis since the early 1990s. What began in closets and greenhouses during prohibition grew into decades of expertise and a legacy of world-renowned genetics, including the legendary White Fire OG.

Today, they are celebrated for award-winning cultivars and a respected, family-owned business with deep roots in California. With a proven track record and global recognition, The Cannabis Brothers have successfully expanded into international markets, including Canada, Australia and Israel.

Slaps
Slaps

Culture-driven.
Experience-led.

Founded in-house, Slaps is a culture-driven cannabis brand built to deliver high-quality products to the market through a simplified, experience-led lens. It's a focused system built around flavour, format, and consistency, rooted in the proven genetic lineage of the Cannabis Brothers.

Slaps combines premium inputs with intentional product formats across pre-rolls, concentrates, and vape systems. Designed to resonate with both seasoned consumers and new entrants.
